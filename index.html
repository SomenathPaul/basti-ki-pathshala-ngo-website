About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala is a youth-led non-profit initiative dedicated to empowering underprivileged children through access to quality education, moral development, and life skills training.

Founded on the belief that education is a basic right, we create learning spaces in underserved communities. Our volunteers teach literacy, numeracy, health, and values to children in slums. Through educational sessions and cultural activities, we nurture confidence and inspire brighter futures.
Our mission: To build a sustainable ecosystem of free and inclusive education, where no child is left behind due to financial or societal constraints.
